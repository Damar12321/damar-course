English Tenses

Master the different types of tenses to speak and write more fluently.

Types of English Tenses

Simple Present

Formula: Subject + Verb 1 (s/es)

Example: She plays the piano every day.

Present Continuous

Formula: Subject + is/am/are + Verb-ing

Example: I am learning English now.

Simple Past

Formula: Subject + Verb 2

Example: They watched a movie last night.

Past Continuous

Formula: Subject + was/were + Verb-ing

Example: He was sleeping at 10 PM.

Simple Future

Formula: Subject + will + Verb 1

Example: We will visit grandma tomorrow.

Future Continuous

Formula: Subject + will be + Verb-ing

Example: She will be working at 9 AM.
